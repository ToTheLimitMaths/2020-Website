About To The Limit Maths

We are a non-profit group of passionate mathematicians that all enjoy doing and teaching math. We want to bring this interest outside of our communities to teach the world about math.

We believe that every student – in elemetary, middle, & high school – should have access to a free and impactful education, and aim to enrich a society of young problem solvers.

To the Limit Maths is run by a team of high schoolers in the Bay Area. We are completely nonprofit and teach for free. All of our tutors have experience with both school and competition math, and have taken the courses they teach before, so they have what it takes to help you succeed. Sign up today!

Our Mission

Our goal at To The Limit Maths is to make the art of mathematics and problem solving approachable and enjoyable to all. We seek to make students both learn and have fun while problem solving. Plus, it's free!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who runs To The Limit?

    To the limit maths is run by our team of students, all in high school around the Bay Area. View our team here!

  • How can I be a tutor?

    Sign up on the Google Form above and we can schedule an interview!

  • Is it free to be tutored?

    Yes, we are a nonprofit, dedicated to helping you!

  • What math levels do you teach?

    We teach elementary school math through pre-calculus.

  • What experience do tutors have?

    Our tutors have experience in many types of math, both in school and competitive math.

  • Do you tutor competition math?

    We have a few tutors who can teach lower level competition math such as AMC 8. We are possibly looking to extend into the AMC 10/12 and AIME.

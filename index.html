About To The Limit Math

We are a non-profit group of passionate mathematicians that all enjoy doing and teaching math. We want to bring this interest outside of our communities to teach the world about math.

We believe that every student – in elementary, middle, & high school – should have access to a free and impactful education, and aim to enrich a society of young problem solvers.

To the Limit Math is run by a team of high schoolers in the Bay Area. We are completely nonprofit and teach for free. All of our tutors have experience with both school and competition math, and have taken the courses they teach before, so they have what it takes to help you succeed. Sign up today!

Our Mission

Our goal at To The Limit Math is to make the art of mathematics and problem solving approachable and enjoyable to all. We seek to make students both learn and have fun while problem solving.

What we Offer

Lessons by the book

We have hand made lessons, lectures, and more built by us using our resources and offical mathematics textbooks, like aops's textbooks. We have found our curriculum one found in accelerated and competitive classes.

Math Tutoring

Currently, we offer math tutoring for all elementary, middle, and some high schoolers, including mathematics up to precalc. (Sign up here to become a tutor). All of our tutors are in high school and have a strong background in math.

Hand-Crafted Curriculums

While we do try to follow our own curriculum, we also provide occasional diagnostics to modify our curriculum to make is as understanding as possible. This allows us to balance the pace and understanding with everyone.

All Digital

In light of the COVID-19 Pandemic, all of our tutoring sessions are digital! We teach through applications that best work for you in order to create the best experience, including Zoom, Discord, Google Meet, and more.
